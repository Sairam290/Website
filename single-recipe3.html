LEMON RICE

LEMON RICE is a simple and delightful dish that combines the zesty brightness of lemon with the bold kick of MIRCHI to enhance the flavor of LEMON RICE. The dish is quick to prepare and offers a perfect balance of citrusy and spicy notes, making it a popular choice for a light and flavorful meal. It's a versatile recipe that can be easily customized to suit your taste preferences, and it pairs well with various side dishes,from rice to vegetables, for a complete and satisfying dining experience.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

6 servings

food

instructions

step 1

Prepare the FRESH LEMON JUICE (e.g., tilapia or cod).

step 2

PREPARE A WHITE RICE AND LET IT DRY

step 3

Preheat a pan over medium-high heat with some oil.

step 4

FRY DALL AND CONEIMENTS A LITTLE UPTO BROWN COLOUR

step 5

POUR LEMON JUICE INTO IT

step 6

MIX IT AND ADD FRIEND CONIENMENTS AND MIX IT WELL

step 4

Serve hot and enjoy!

ingredients

4 LEMON

1 teaspoon DALL

2 teaspoon powdered cumin

3 chopped,de seeded green chilli

1 tablespoon virgin olive oil

2 large FRIED CONIEMENTS

1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic

1 cinnamon

1 teaspoon powdered black pepper

1 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice

1 tablespoon salt

tools

Frying Pan or Skillet

Olive Oil or Cooking Spray

Plate or Platter,Lemon Squeezer

Zester or Microplane
