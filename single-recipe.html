LEMON PEPPER FISH

Lemon Pepper Fish is a simple and delightful dish that combines the zesty brightness of lemon with the bold kick of black pepper to enhance the flavor of mild white fish. The dish is quick to prepare and offers a perfect balance of citrusy and spicy notes, making it a popular choice for a light and flavorful meal. It's a versatile recipe that can be easily customized to suit your taste preferences, and it pairs well with various side dishes,from rice to vegetables, for a complete and satisfying dining experience.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

6 servings

food

instructions

step 1

Prepare the fish fillets (e.g., tilapia or cod).

step 2

Season both sides with lemon pepper seasoning, salt, and a touch of olive oil.

step 3

Preheat a pan over medium-high heat with some oil.

step 4

Cook the fish for 3-4 minutes on each side or until it's golden brown and cooked through.

step 5

Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the fish.

step 6

Garnish with lemon zest and fresh parsley

step 4

Serve hot and enjoy!

ingredients

4 fish

1 teaspoon finely chopped ginger

2 teaspoon powdered cumin

3 chopped,de seeded green chilli

1 tablespoon virgin olive oil

2 large finely chopped onion

1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic

1 cinnamon

1 teaspoon powdered black pepper

1 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice

1 tablespoon salt

tools

Frying Pan or Skillet

Olive Oil or Cooking Spray

Plate or Platter,Lemon Squeezer

Zester or Microplane
