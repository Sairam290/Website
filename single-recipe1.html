BUTTER CHICKEN

Butter Chicken, also known as "Murgh Makhani," is a popular and indulgent Indian dish. It consists of tender chicken pieces marinated in a flavorful mixture of yogurt and spices, then simmered in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. The dish is characterized by its harmonious blend of spices, tomato sweetness, and the luxurious creaminess of butter and heavy cream. It's often served with naan bread or rice, making it a beloved and comforting dish enjoyed by people around the world.

prep time

40 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

6 servings

food

instructions

step 1

Marinate chicken pieces in yogurt, lemon juice, and spices.

step 2

Grill or cook the chicken until it's partially cooked.

step 3

In a separate pan, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until golden.

step 4

Add tomato sauce and spices, cooking until oil separates.

step 5

Stir in heavy cream and butter, creating a creamy sauce.

step 6

Add the partially cooked chicken to the sauce.

step 4

Simmer until chicken is cooked through and sauce is rich and flavorful and serve it.
